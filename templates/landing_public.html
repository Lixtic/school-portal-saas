Academic Engine

Auto-calculate every grade. Generate every report.

Enter raw class scores and exam scores — SchoolPortal does the rest. It computes weighted totals, assigns letter grades (A–F), writes remarks, and generates polished PDF report cards in one click. Supports GES Primary, JHS and SHS formats.

  • Weighted scoring — configurable class score vs. exam percentages per subject.
  • One-click PDF reports — individual student or full class report cards, ready to print.
  • Grade analytics — class averages, subject performance trends, top performers.

Finance Module

Know exactly who has paid. And who hasn't.

Build fee structures (tuition, PTA, capitation) per class and term, then bulk-assign bills to every student. As payments come in, the system auto-updates balances and generates receipt numbers — no spreadsheets involved.

  • Auto-generated receipts — every payment gets a unique reference like RCP-20260319-A7X2.
  • Bulk fee assignment — one click assigns fees from a template to an entire class.
  • Parent-visible balances — parents see their child's fee status in real-time via the portal.
