What We Are

A complete, AI-enhanced SaaS platform transforming education management for Ghanaian schools

SchoolPadi is a production-grade multi-tenant platform built on Django 5 with schema-based data isolation via PostgreSQL. Every school gets its own isolated database schema, dedicated subscription tier, and AI capabilities — all sharing a single hosted infrastructure.

AI Availability

Role-Based Access

Admins, Teachers, Students, and Parents each get dedicated dashboards, scoped views, and granular permission control.

Academic Management

Academic years, class/subject management, timetable generation, auto-calculated grades (class score + exams), and attendance tracking.

Finance & Fees

Fee head definitions, per-student fee assignment, Paystack checkout for online payments, automated parent email reminders, and scholarship adjustments.

Parent Portal

Parents view all children's grades, attendance (30-day), homework submissions, SchoolPadi AI stats, and a live fee summary — all from one dashboard.

Duty Roster

Auto-generate term duty weeks, assign teachers, personalised "on duty this week" banners, and a print-to-PDF roster with full dark mode support.

Enterprise Security

Schema-level data isolation, CSRF protection, HMAC-verified Paystack webhooks, trial enforcement middleware, and OWASP-aligned access control.

Homework & Reports

Digital homework assignments, student submission tracking with scoring, and PDF report card generation via ReportLab with school branding.

Announcements & Push

School-wide announcements, per-user notifications with unread badge, parent-to-admin messaging, and PWA push notification infrastructure.

Dark Mode & PWA

System-wide dark mode with per-page overrides (including standalone pages), localStorage persistence, OS preference detection, and installable PWA manifest.
