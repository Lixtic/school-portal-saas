What We Are

A comprehensive, AI-enhanced platform transforming education management

School Portal SaaS is a cutting-edge multi-tenant school management system built specifically for Ghanaian educational institutions. Powered by Django 5 and advanced AI technology, we provide each school with complete data isolation through PostgreSQL schema-based multi-tenancy, ensuring privacy and security while sharing infrastructure costs.

User Management

Complete role-based access for Admins, Teachers, Students, and Parents with granular permissions and customizable profiles.

Academic Management

Full academic year cycles, automated timetable generation, comprehensive grade management with auto-calculation, and attendance tracking.

Financial Operations

Multi-tier fee structures, automated billing, payment tracking, scholarship management, and financial reporting.

Communication Hub

School-wide announcements, parent portal for progress monitoring, homework distribution, and integrated messaging.

Subscription System

Four-tier plans with universal add-on marketplace. All users can browse features while admins control purchases.

Enterprise Security

Multi-layered access control, CSRF protection, schema-aware authentication, and complete data sovereignty per school.
