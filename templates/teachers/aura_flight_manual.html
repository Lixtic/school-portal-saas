The Morning Sync
Don't start from scratch. Let Aura-T do the heavy lifting.
Prep · 2 mins
⚡ Action
Tap "Generate Draft" in your Lesson Planner. Select your class, subject, and topic — Aura-T drafts the full 45-minute plan in under 30 seconds.
No blank page. Every plan comes pre-loaded with a Localized Hook, Pulse Check diagnostics, dual learning paths, and a Mastery Sprint.
🧠 The Secret
Aura-T has already scanned your students' mastery baseline. If they struggled with Fractions last week, today's Algebra plan automatically includes a refresher in Phase 1's Pulse Check.
The Learner DNA block at the top tells you the one prior-concept gap most likely to surface — read it before class.
✅ Check
Before leaving the staffroom, confirm:
GES Indicator matches your Scheme of Work (e.g., B7.2.1.1)
Localized Hook names a real Ghanaian place, person, or practice your class will recognise
📲
Phase 1: The Digital Pulse
Stop asking "Does everyone understand?" — start seeing the data.
0 – 5 mins
⚡ Action
Switch to Aura-T View on your tablet. Read the three Pulse Check questions aloud (or project them). Students raise hands, show whiteboards, or tap their response.
Tap 📲 Digital Pulse to display the questions as full-screen interactive cards — no login required for students.
👁️ The Look
Your screen shows the three questions formatted as True / False + Open cards. Mentally log — or physically note — which students hesitate on Q1 or Q2.
Q1 targets the prerequisite gap.
Q2 targets the most common misconception.
Q3 tests real-world transfer.
🚦 The Gate
If <50 % of students answer Q1 correctly — STOP. Do not move to Phase 2.
Use a Market Analogy to close the prerequisite gap immediately. Two minutes of re-teaching now saves twenty minutes of confusion later.
🧭
Phase 2: Co-Pilot Mode
Stop lecturing. Start mentoring. The room is your cockpit.
5 – 35 mins
⚡ Action
Move around the room. Tablet in hand. Students who hesitated on Q1/Q2 are now on the Support Path — structured steps, sentence frames, and a peer partner. Students who aced the Pulse Check are on the Extension Path (Junior Innovator).

Check progress with the three Checkpoint Questions — read them aloud at the midpoint. The Heatmap tells you Red / Amber / Green per student.
🟥 The Heatmap
Your screen shows the 🟢 Green Tiles — leave them; Aura is feeding them Extension Challenges.

🔴 Red / Amber Tiles are your priority. Walk directly to those desks. Aura has already flagged the specific misconception (e.g., "Kofi is confused about the variable").
Rule: never shout across the room. Every student who sees you walking toward them knows you care.
🏆 The Pro Move
Use the "Push Hint" button to send a visual clue to a struggling group without saying a word — so you don't disrupt the students already in flow.
Bimodal Delivery view gives you the exact Teacher Script for each path — what to say, what to write on the board, step by step. Print the Student Nugget cards and hand them face-down before class.
🏁
Phase 3: The Evidence
No more marking 40 notebooks at home. The data comes to you.
35 – 45 mins
⚡ Action
Call "Mastery Sprint."
Students work solo — no hints, no discussion. Three targeted questions: MS1 Recall  MS2 Concept  MS3 Application.

Distribute the printed ✂ Student Cards (cut-and-distribute strip) or project the exit questions from the Aura-T view.
📊 The Data
The 🤖 Teacher Insight card updates automatically. It tells you:
At-Risk Profile — who will need 1-on-1 tomorrow
Tomorrow's Action — the exact first 5-minute intervention
Monitor Signals — what to watch for at the door
📣 The Wrap-Up Script
Read Aura-T's "Tomorrow's Focus" to the class before the bell:
"Great job today! Tomorrow, we tackle [Tomorrow's Focus topic] — that's where we tripped up today. Come ready."
Students leave knowing exactly what's next. No ambiguity. No homework surprises.
⭐ The Golden Rules of Aura-T
📡
Trust the Data
If Aura flags a student in Red, believe it. They are stuck, even if they look fine. The data doesn't lie.
🚶
Stay Mobile
The Command Center is on your phone/tablet so you can be with your students — not behind a desk. The desk is for marking. The floor is for teaching.
🌍
Localize
Aura-T provides the Hook, but you provide the Soul. Change the analogies to fit your town, your students' parents' jobs, your classroom jokes. The AI gives you the scaffold; you add the culture.
