Our Story

Built for schools that deserve better tools.

Portals is an AI-powered school management platform designed specifically for African education — starting in Ghana, built to scale.

Why Portals exists

School administrators across Africa spend hours each week on work that should take minutes — entering marks into spreadsheets, chasing fee payments, coordinating timetables by hand. Teachers plan lessons on paper, often without access to curriculum resources or data on which students are falling behind.

Portals was built to change that. We started with a simple question: what would a school management system look like if it were designed from the ground up for Ghanaian schools, with AI built in from day one?

"Every feature in Portals traces back to a real conversation with a headmaster, a class teacher, or a bursary officer. We build what they actually need."

The result is a platform that handles grades, attendance, fees, timetables, lesson planning, and parent communication — all in one place, all working together.

What makes Portals different

Built for Africa
GES curriculum alignment, BECE and WASSCE grading, and fee structures that match how Ghanaian schools actually operate.
AI That Actually Helps
Aura-T generates lesson plans, slides, and assignments in seconds. It flags struggling students and writes their report remarks automatically.
School-Level Isolation
Every school runs in its own data schema. Your student records, grades, and fees are completely separate from every other school on the platform.

Our mission

To give every school in Africa — regardless of size or budget — access to the same quality of management tools that the best-resourced institutions in the world use. We measure success not in features shipped, but in hours saved for teachers and admins who can now spend that time where it matters most.

  • Free tier available for schools with fewer than 100 students
  • No hardware required — works on any device with a browser
  • Built with offline-first design principles for low-connectivity environments
  • Priced in local currency with flexible payment terms
