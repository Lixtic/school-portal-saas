Frequently Asked Questions

What is the NaCCA curriculum?
The National Council for Curriculum and Assessment (NaCCA) develops Ghana's national curriculum framework. It defines strands, sub-strands, content standards, and performance indicators for every subject from KG to SHS.
Which grades does SchoolPadi cover?
We currently cover Basic 7, Basic 8, and Basic 9 (JHS 1–3) across all 8 core subjects — Mathematics, Science, English, Social Studies, RME, Computing, Creative Arts & Design, and Career Technology.
Is this free to use?
Browsing the curriculum is completely free with no account required. You also get one free AI lesson plan generation per tool. For unlimited AI generations, credits start from GH₵2.
How accurate are the curriculum indicators?
Our curriculum database is extracted directly from official GES/NaCCA PDF documents using AI-assisted parsing, then manually verified. We currently have 1,600+ performance indicators across all subjects and grade levels.
Can I use this for lesson planning?
Absolutely. Select any performance indicator and click "Generate Lesson Plan" — SchoolPadi's AI will create a complete, NaCCA-aligned lesson plan with objectives, activities, resources, and assessment strategies.
