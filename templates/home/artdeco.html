Foundational Architecture

The Three Pillars

Three jewelled systems. One unified institution.

I
Academic Intelligence

A powerful academic engine. Every grade is computed, weighted, and ranked — then turned into a clean official report card, automatically.

  • Weighted grade computation (class + exam + homework)
  • AI-generated remarks and grade narrative
  • PDF report cards — individual or full class
  • Term rankings, subject averages, trend charts
  • National grading systems (BECE, WASSCE, IGCSE)
II
Operational Precision

Full visibility over attendance, timetables, and staff. Every teacher, every class, every school day — organised and tracked with precision.

  • Daily attendance with mobile roll-call interface
  • Monthly heatmap analytics per student
  • Visual timetable builder with conflict detection
  • Faculty profiles, duty rosters, lesson plans
  • AI homework generation and auto-marking engine
III
Fiscal Architecture

Complete control over school finances. Fee structures are set per class, billed to each student, and receipted automatically — with balances visible to parents in real time.

  • Configurable fee heads — tuition, PTA, uniform, bus
  • One-click bulk assignment from class templates
  • Auto-generated receipts (RCP-YYYYMMDD-XXXX)
  • Payment history with instant parent visibility
  • Outstanding balance reports by class or term
