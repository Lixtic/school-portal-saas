Common questions
How long does setup take?
Most schools are live within five minutes. You create the account, enter your school details, and the platform is ready. Adding students and teachers can be done via CSV import or one by one.
Is there a free plan?
Yes. Schools with fewer than 100 students can use the core features — grades, attendance, and fees — at no cost. AI features like Aura-T and the Pulse Check require a paid plan.
Can I migrate data from another system?
Yes. We support CSV import for students, teachers, grades, and fee records. For larger migrations, our support team can assist directly.
Is our school data private?
Completely. Each school runs in its own isolated database schema. No other school can access your data, and we never share it with third parties.
